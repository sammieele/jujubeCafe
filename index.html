ABOUT JUJUBE

Jujube Cafe was founded in 2017 by Sammie Le with the intention of bringing Asian-style artistian goodies to California. Sammie has crafted and perfected many Asian-inspired baked goods, receiving great applaud by food conniseurs throughout the world. Jujube Cafe prides itself on our reliability, surprises and taste. In addition to our ever-evolving baked goods and bubble tea menu, we serve fresh made-to-order lunchbox cakes and catering.

"We promise fresh baked and mouth-watering desserts to your hands till midnight everyday." Late nights aren't so bad if you have something sweet.

Baker, Bobrarista and Owner: Sammie Le

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 12am (we support our fellow students!)

Address: 275 Jujube Blvd, CA
